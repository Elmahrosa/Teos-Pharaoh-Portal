## Purpose
The TEOS Foundation is designed as a **non-profit public benefit entity**
to steward the TEOS Egypt ecosystem.

It ensures **neutral governance, regulatory compliance, and continuity**.

---

## Jurisdictional Alignment
Designed for compatibility with:
- EU Non-Profit Foundations
- MENA Public Benefit Entities
- International NGO frameworks

---

## Foundation Responsibilities
- Custody of protocol IP (non-exclusive)
- Oversight of DAO governance
- Treasury supervision
- Grant and ecosystem funding
- Legal and regulatory compliance

---

## Relationship with DAO
- DAO governs protocol evolution
- Foundation enforces legal boundaries
- Neither may override the Constitution alone

---

## Board Structure (Proposed)
- Founder seat (initial, time-limited)
- Independent compliance expert
- Community-elected representative
- External auditor (observer)

---

## Financial Integrity
- Annual independent audits
- Public financial disclosures
- Restricted-use treasury categories

---

**The Foundation protects the mission  
so the DAO can protect the future.**
